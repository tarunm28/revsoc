The Cascades Neopolis is situated in Kokapet, Hyderabad, within Neopolis. It is five minutes from ORR Exit 1A and twenty-five minutes from RGIA International Airport.

The Cascades Neopolis offers 1,189 intelligently planned 3 and 4 BHK residences, along with 10 exclusive triplex penthouses with private pools. Configurations range from 2,560 sq ft for a 3 BHK to 4,825 sq ft for a 4 BHK.

Residences at The Cascades Neopolis start at ₹3.5 Cr.

Located at the heart of the property, the lagoon-style swimming pool is surrounded by greenery and serves as a cocoon for exercise and relaxation. It is set 75 feet above ground level, on the podium, and is enhanced by lap pools, jacuzzi pools, floating daybeds, cabanas, and interactive water play areas. A kids' pool sits alongside, where children can enjoy a swim safely.

The podium level sits 75 feet above the ground and houses the lagoon-style swimming pool, landscaped gardens, a walking trail, an amphitheatre, multipurpose lawns, and cricket, basketball and pickleball practice courts. It is designed as an open and sheltered space for you to unwind, with a courtyard view of the pool and floating pods visible from your balcony.

The 63rd floor is home to the Sky Garden, the largest of its kind in Neopolis at 92,000 sq ft. It includes a rooftop infinity pool, a temperature-controlled pool, a sky lounge, a sky bar, BBQ areas, a gym, sunken seating with water features, and two helipads. A sky bridge connects all five towers at this height, and a panoramic glass elevator carries you up with views of the Outer Ring Road on the way.

The clubhouse is a grand 8-storey world for socialising, exercising and relaxation, set across 50,000 sq ft. It houses an all-day dining restaurant, sports lounge and games, a mini theatre, the spa, a multipurpose hall, Pilates and yoga studios, a squash court, gym, badminton court, and an outdoor paddle tennis court.

Under each tower, you will find a guest lounge and library, gaming zone, kids' play area, crèche, indoor games, art and pottery studio, multipurpose rooms, coworking spaces, and guest rooms for your visitors, along with an outdoor gym and outdoor mini golf.

The spa at The Cascades Neopolis is managed by L'Occitane, the French luxury brand. The wellness floor at the clubhouse offers a juice bar, salt bath, foot reflexology, waiting lounge, sauna, and a hammam at the Sky Lounge. Boxing, martial arts and strength training facilities sit on the floor above.

Yes, The Cascades Neopolis is registered with the Telangana State RERA under registration number P02400009538.
